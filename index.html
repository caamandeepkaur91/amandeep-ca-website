About the Firm

A local practice, run like a proper institution.

Amandeep Kaur & Co. is a Chartered Accountancy firm based near the Bus Stand in Zira, District Ferozepur, Punjab, established in March 2016. Over the years, the firm has grown into a one-stop compliance partner for individuals, traders, MSMEs and companies across the district.

From routine GST returns to complex appeals before tax authorities, from registering a new LLP to renewing an FSSAI licence, the firm is built to handle the full lifecycle of a business's statutory and financial obligations — so clients deal with one trusted office instead of five different consultants.

Every engagement is handled with the same discipline a ledger demands: accurate, on time, and accounted for.

What We Do

Services, entered line by line.

Every service the firm offers, organised the way a chart of accounts would list them — by code, not by chance.

01 GST Advisory & Compliance Registration, monthly/quarterly returns, reconciliation, and advisory on rate classification and ITC eligibility.
02 Income Tax Filing & Planning ITR filing for individuals, firms and companies, tax planning, and computation review under both tax regimes.
03 TDS / TCS Compliance Return filing, correction statements, certificate generation, and advisory on deduction and collection obligations.
04 Statutory & Tax Audits Audit of accounts under the Income Tax Act and Companies Act, with reporting that stands up to scrutiny.

09 Other Trade Licensing Assistance with additional municipal and trade licences required to operate legally in your line of business.
10 Appeals — Income Tax, TDS & GST Representation and drafting for appeals and rectifications before tax authorities across all three verticals.
